A HTML5 website using JavaScript and JQuery to play games.

1. Tic Tac Toe:
One or two players place either X's or O's on the board and try to place three in a row to score. Have to be able to understand two dimensial board. Ages 5 and up.

2. Dress Up Game:
Slide clothing on top of the person to give them the look you like. Ages 3 and up.

3. 2048:
Drag identical numbers together in order to make them add up to 2048 to win the game.

Honor's Project: Memory:
Flip cards to reveal the content and try to match them up before the time runs out.
